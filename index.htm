Kanji Voice Quiz

A more natural way to practice Japanese Kanji readings: simply speak the phoenetic translation of the character shown.

This is a work in progress that makes use of the experimental Web Speech API, and so currently only works on Chrome. Make sure to allow access to the microphone; a headset is recommended.

Made by Elliot Bentley. Technical people are welcome to contribute to development on GitHub.
